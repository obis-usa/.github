# Governance

OBIS-USA follows a [Consensus](https://producingoss.com/html-chunk/consensus-democracy.html) governance structure.
Wherein, each agreement is settled by seeking consensus where everyone is willing to live with the decision.

More details to follow as the community evolves.

Roles:
   * **Node Manager** - Supports data holders, standardizes marine biodiversity records, and publishes datasets to the global OBIS platform.
   * **Staff** - Supports the Node Manager in all activities
   * **Community Member** - Not necessarily associated with the Node, but contributes to Node activities.
   * **Data Provider** - researchers, institutions, and local, national or regional biodiversity monitoring programs which produce data.
